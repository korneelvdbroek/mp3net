MP3net

MP3net is deep convolutional GAN which leverages techniques from MP3/Vorbis audio compression to produce long, high-quality audio tracks with long-range coherence.

The model uses a Modified Discrete Cosine Transform (MDCT) data representation which includes all phase information. Phase generation is hence integral part of the model. We leverage the auditory masking and psychoacoustic perception limit of the human ear to widen the true distribution and reduce the model training time. The model architecture is a deep convolutional net where each subsequent model block stretches the data along the time axis and adds a higher octave. The deeper layers are connected with all parts of the output and have the context of the full track. This results in long-range coherence.

The samples presented here are after 105h training on a single TPUv2. At this stage, the model has not yet converged and sample quality is still steadily improving.
